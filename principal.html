Forewords from Principal



I believe that education shapes the character, calibre and prospect of an individual. Education has no perimeter to restrict. Intensive and critical thinking, sharpening intelligence, refining character – all are a part of dignified education. Students of ABC Public School are benefitted by such type of educstion.
I see that the journey to the future essentially needs a glimpse of the past. An examination of the annals of 27 years shows the glittering milestones and fluttering flags of triumphs as a testimony to Viswajyothis’s dynamic existence. Each milestone has a glorious story to tell, each flag has a victory song to intone.
I am sure that the students, whether the current batch or the alumni, enjoy the disciplined freedom, refined culture, God fearing compassion, boundless opportunities, healthy friendship, limitless affection and colourful celebrations that guide them to be perfectly gentle human beings.
Wishing you all a great Academic Session 2021-2022!!

Mr. William Maruthella
Principal
ABC public school
Toronto
